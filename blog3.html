CARDIO

Rev up your heart rate, improve endurance, and burn calories with cardio exercises. Stay consistent, track progress, and feel the cardio high.

Incorporate plyometric exercises: Plyometric exercises like box jumps and medicine ball tosses activate metabolism and increase elastic strength with fast and short actions, improving muscle power.

Perform isokinetic exercises: Isokinetic exercises, such as repetitive movements at a constant speed, enhance muscular power.

Combine heavier resistance sets with lighter speed-based movements: This enhances performance and develops strength.

Consult an expert or physical trainer: If you have any doubts about managing your training, consult an expert or physical trainer who can guide you.
